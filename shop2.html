Wisdom of Dreaming: A Guide to an Effective Dream Life | Paul M. Sheldon, M.A. with Elizabeth Eagar, M.A.

Becoming more open to your dreams can be one of the most rewarding experiences of your life. Sometimes dreams are terrifying, but they don’t have to stay that way! Deepening and expanding your relationship with your own dream life allows you to learn more about your daily life, aspects you want to explore and aspects you don’t want to explore. Dreamosophy, literally, the wisdom of dreaming, approaches dreams and dreaming differently from other traditions. The activities and exercises in this guidebook can help you find confluences between dreaming and waking, and can help you make the changes you long for, to have your life become a dream come true.
